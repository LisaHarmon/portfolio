## About Me

Hello and welcome! I'm Lisa, a technical writer with 14 years of experience in authoring, editing, and maintaining content for technical and nontechnical audiences. I developed this site to showcase my professional skills. With a background that spans enterprise software, operational processes and controls, and developer support documentation, I bring a diverse skill set to the teams I support and partner with. I've led large-scale documentation projects, including initiatives that strengthened writing standards and workflows, and helped teams position documentation as an organizational asset.
