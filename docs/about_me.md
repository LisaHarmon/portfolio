# More About Me
---

## My Experience

I’m a technical writer with 14 years of experience creating clear, structured content that helps people understand complex systems and complete tasks with confidence. Over the course of my career, I’ve partnered with engineers, product teams, and business stakeholders to transform technical concepts and workflows into content that is practical, usable, and accessible to a wide range of audiences.

I love what I do for the opportunity to continually learn and grow. Few things evolve as quickly as technology, and I thrive in environments where I’m always building new knowledge, refining my skills, and challenging myself to better understand the tools, systems, and processes behind the content I create.

## My Approach

I believe I create my best content when I'm involved early and often in the process. I actively seek opportunities to be involved in product discussions and planning so I can understand not just what needs to be documented, but how it works and why it matters.

I approach every project as a “first user.” By immersing myself in an application, tool, or workflow, I experience it the way a user would. This perspective helps me ask better questions, uncover gaps or inconsistencies, and identify potential points of confusion before they reach the end user. My goal is to remove friction and ensure that the path forward is clear, intuitive, and efficient.

## My Impact

Throughout my career, I’ve led large-scale documentation initiatives that strengthened writing standards, improved workflows, and elevated documentation as a valuable organizational asset. I’ve developed and maintained content for both technical and nontechnical audiences, adapting my approach to meet the needs of each group.

I've helped organizations develop and improve their:

* process and workflow documentation,
* knowledge base systems,
* onboarding and training materials, and
* technical and user-facing documentation.

Across all these areas, my focus remains the same: creating content that helps people succeed.

## My Perspective

I see content as a bridge connecting the people who design and build systems with the people who rely on them to do their jobs. That perspective drives me to deeply understand both sides: the system itself and the needs, goals, and challenges of the user.

Whether I’m documenting an operational workflow, building a knowledge base, or supporting application users, I aim to create content that is not just accurate but genuinely helpful.

## Why This Matters

At its core, my work is about helping people accomplish their goals. Clear, thoughtful content reduces frustration, builds confidence, and enables users to move forward without hesitation. When users succeed, the business gains trust. Ultimately, user-focused content helps businesses achieve their strategic goals.

My hope is that this portfolio reflects not only my experience and skill set, but also my commitment to creating user-friendly content that helps people and businesses get where they need to go.
