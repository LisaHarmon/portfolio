# Quick Start Guide
---

**Once you [install and set up Smart Shopper]({% link docs/samples/software/user_guide_install_setup_ss.md %}), start using the app in three steps:**

#### Step 1: Create your first shopping list

1. Tap **Lists**.
2. Select **New List**, enter your list name, and then tap **Create**.
3. When the new list appears, tap **Add an item**.
4. Search for the item, or use the barcode scanner.
5. Tap the item you want to add. Your list appears and displays the item you added.
6. When you finish adding your list items, [compare prices](#step-2-compare-prices) to choose the best store.

#### Step 2: Compare prices

1. Tap **Compare Stores**, and then select the shopping list you want to use. A list of available stores appears.
2. Select up to three stores, and then tap **Continue**. A grid appears and displays your items, each item's price at your chosen stores, and the store total for your list. If an **x** appears for the price, the item is out of stock at that store.
3. Review the item details and store totals to choose the best store. When you arrive at the store, [use the store map](#step-3-use-the-store-map) to locate the items on your shopping list.

#### Step 3: Use the store map

1. When you arrive at the store, go to the list you want to shop.
2. Tap **Store Map**. An interactive store map appears, showing a highlighted route and the location of each list item.
3. Follow the highlighted route through the store aisles.
